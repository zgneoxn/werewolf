Civilians

Consists of villager and special roles

Villager

The Villager has no special powers. Their goal is to identify and vote out the werewolves. Villagers work together during the day to figure out who is lying and protect their own.

Seer

The Seer is a crucial player on the villager team. Each night, the Seer can secretly discover if one player is a werewolf. The Seer must share this information carefully to avoid being targeted by wolves.

Witch

The Witch has two potions during the whole of one game: a heal potion, and a poison potion. Whilst the witch still has the heal potion, they will be able to know who the werewolfs attacked that night, and hence be able to choose whether to use the heal potion to save them or not. The poison potion allows the witch to kill someone during the night. Both potions can only be used once each game (not night), and only one potion can be used each night. The witch can only heal herself during the first night if she is attacked.

Hunter

The Hunter is a deadly ally for the villagers. If the Hunter is eliminated, they can take down another player with them. The hunter cannot activate his ability if he died from the witch's poison.

Knight

The Knight is a. He has a powerful one-time ability which he can activate anytime during the day before voting - to reveal his identity and challenge one other player. If that player is a wolf, the challenged player instantly dies and the day ends and enters into night phase without voting. If the challenged player is part of the civilian team, the knight himself dies day phase and voting continues.

Wolves

Includes normal werewolf and other special wolves

Werewolf

The Werewolf is part of the werewolf team. They try to eliminate villagers at night, while pretending to be innocent during the day. The wolves need to keep their identity hidden to avoid being voted out.

Alpha Wolf

The Alpha Wolf is a special wolf role. When he dies during the day or night (and not a result of the witch's poison), he will be able to activate his ability to remove one player from the game.
